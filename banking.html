Banking App

This is a banking app UX/UI design project that I worked on. The app can be used to do a variety of tasks such as checking a balance, making a transfer and saving money. One of the challenges of designing a banking app is balancing convenience with security.

User Research

User Research Goals

  • Understand user goals when using this type of website
  • Understand user pain points when using websites
  • Understand what type of tasks users carry out on these types of websites

Surveys and Interviews

For the quantitative research, I carried out a survey to find out about what users expect from a banking app. For the qualitative research, I interviewed people who had experience with a online banking.

Key Insights from the User Research

  • Users would like to save money for specific things
  • Users would like to easily transfer money
  • Users would like to see a tick showing that their payment has been successful
  • Users would like the option of seeing a calendar view of their transactions
  • Users would like to see graphs giving them insights into their income and spending
  • Users would like the option of easily contact the bank

User Stories

Signing in and out

  • As a user I want to securely sign in and out of the app
  • As a user I want to know when I have signed out of the app

Making a transfer

  • As a user I want to easily transfer money between my accounts
  • As a user I want to make payments easily and securely
  • As a user I want to see that the money transfer has been successful

Tracking income and expenses

  • As a user I want to get insights into my spending habits
  • As a user I want to see a calendar view of my income and expenses

Saving money

  • As a user I want to save money for specific purchases
  • As a user I want to see how much money I've saved for a purchase
